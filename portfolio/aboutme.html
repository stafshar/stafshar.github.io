About Me

I am currently a Masters of Science student specializing in UX Research and Design at the University of Michigan’s School of Information.

I am a problem solver who is passionate about designing products that are driven by users’ needs through innovative and empathic design practices. I am interested in how implicit bias, the visceral reaction we have to individuals and stimuli in our environment, subconsciously affects both everyday decision making as well as the critical design decisions made during digital product development.

I am committed to the reflective design philosophy as a foundation to better understand how bias fits into the narrative of User Experience research and design so that I can lead teams in developing products that are not only competitive, but also accessible and inclusive.
